Don't Die. Ingredify!

If you or your relatives suffer from allergies, you deserve a quick and easy way of finding out if you can savely consume a product. Wasting more than 50% of your shopping time decoding packaging labels is not the way we are thinking of. Even worse, some allergens remain overseen and can lead to allergic reactions up to an anaphilactic shock, which can lead - to death. This is where Ingredify comes into play.
Ingredify is the first fully funtional application for allergy sufferers. Easily create your profile by inserting the ingredients you want to avoid or the allergies you have and scan a product.
Our algorithms will rapidly compare the products ingredients to the previously safed profile, and will warn you of a harmful ingredient or give an OK signal!

Completely Free.

We don't want our customers to pay for what should be self evident: To go shopping without squandering time decoding packaging labels. To go shopping without having to fear that an ingredient could seriously harm you. We want to contribute our share to make people who suffer from allergies live a normal life. This is why Ingredify is completely free.
